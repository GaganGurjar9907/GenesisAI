## Gam design document - Crimson Sands (GTA-Style Open World) - A Modern Indian Narrative

**1. Overview:**

* **Game Title:** Crimson Sand
   *  Working title, ready to be refined.
    * Theme: Grand Theft Auto meets Indian mythology and culture blending with modern urban settings, creating a dynamic and vibrant open world experience steeped in colonial history.



2.  **Genre:** Action RPG Open World Simulation (with strong emphasis on player agency)

3. **Setting**: Modern-day India - Mumbai & Rajasthan, overlapping regions with historical influences


4. **Tone:** Gritty realism mixed with lavish visuals, saturated color palatte & cultural quirks, themes of identity, corruption, honor, tradition, and rebellion. Aim for a morally complex environment. Think *Assassin’s Creed Origins* meets *God of War*, but leans heavily into South Asian aesthetics.

5.  **Target Audience**: Fans of open world games like *Grand Theft Auto V/VI,* RPGs with character customization and deep gameplay, players captivated by Bollywood, Indian history & culture, and action enthusiasts desiring immersive gaming experiences.


6. **Platforms:** PC (initial focus), PS5, Xbox Series X/S


7.  **Sales Target**: $20-$30 Million on launch

8.  **Gameplay Pillars – Key Focus Points:**

     *  **Deep Narrative & World-Building:** Explore a richly detailed India through multiple interconnected storylines with rich characters and nuanced narratives. It’s not just about crime, it's an epic saga with ancient secrets. 
     * **Player Customization - Cultural Fusion (with choices):** Allow the player to adopt a particular identity – whether influenced by tradition or rebellion.  This includes clothing styles, religious affiliations, and even subtle linguistic customization that impacts interactions.  Consequence is felt and visible throughout the world.


       *  Influence of Regional Beliefs : Explore variations, regional interpretations & debates surrounding religions & mythological figures 



     * **Action & Combat Mechanics:** Blend established action combat with Indian-inspired styles – focusing on fluid parkour, foot chases, ranged attacks using traditional weapons and spices/herbs for strategic advantage.




        *   **Spice System (Faction Warfare):** Introduce a “spice” economy.  Players could forge alliances through trade to benefit their faction which affects global power dynamics.



      * **Crafting & Alchemy – Resource Gathering:** Deep system for creating potions, items with Indian meanings tied to ancient rituals & traditions; providing multiple pathways to earn rewards, improve character/equipment or influence storylines.




     *   **Social Interaction - "Khalsa" Community (Unique Dialogue System):**  Implement a unique ‘Khalsa’ community system – players could align themselves with various groups and communities based on their interests. Interactions and choices affect reputation across sections of the world and unlock branching narrative paths, affecting faction alliances



        * **Religious Rituals & Festivals:** Incorporate large-scale moments relating to major Hindu/Muslim rites(like Holi, Diwali, Eid) allowing for immersive experiences through timed events - resource gain & influence




9. **Story Outline (High Level):**

    *   **Act I: Foundations – The Colonial Shadow:** Introduction to Mumbai’s stark past and the beginnings of criminal organization tied to the colonial era. Explores societal divisions and the formation of criminal factions vying for dominance. Introduces key characters & historical connections.



         * **Event:**  Uncover a relic of a lost civilization – clues revealed through ancient scrolls, leading player to explore forgotten temples -




    *   **Act I & II: Serpent's Embrace - Conflict of Nations:**  Players will be drawn into multiple city factions representing rival clans/guilds and regional powers. A conspiracy unfolds involving powerful figures hidden in the shadows. Explore a network of underground markets.

       * **The 'Ghatas':** Large-scale gang battles across rural India, exploring territorial control, traditional beliefs vs modern criminality, challenging player morals




    *   **Act II & III: Ancient Echoes –  Myth and Legacy:** Focus on revisiting historical landmarks - Mughal ruins intertwined with tribal customs. Unraveling the history of gods/saints and exploring forbidden ancient rituals impacting contemporary politics  

       * **Fragmented History**: Player discovers fragments of forgotten history, causing turmoil for factions across areas around Mumbai . 


     *   **Epilogue : Crimson Desert** A looming war between factions sets off a cataclysmic shift, emphasizing the importance of preserving India's cultural heritage.



10.  **Art Direction & Visual Style:**

     * **Color Palletes**: Primary color palette based on Indian textiles and hues - Rich colors such as saffron (red), deep blues, jewel-tone greens, and warm oranges, reflecting diverse regions –
        * Color Gradient Effects: Dynamic color transitions throughout levels reflecting India's varied climate



     *   **Architectural Design:** Blends ancient Mughal styles with contemporary urban landscapes. 




11.  **Sound System:** Authentic Indian orchestral score layered with electronic beats and trap music to create an immersive and dynamic soundscape. Dialogue can include a mix of Hindi, English, and subtle accents that change depending on the region.



 **12. Features – Detailed Breakdown (Beyond core gameplay):**

    *   **NPC Dynamic Population/Social Scenarios :** AI-driven NPCs with distinct cultural identities— influenced by region to ensure varied dialogue. 
      *  Branching storylines, character-level impacts - affect overall story and factions involved



      * **Procedural Generation**: Enhance the replayability— for world building. Vary cities - terrain, events, resources dynamically created across the regions based on global circumstances




   *   **Authentic Weather & Environmental Effects :** Implement dynamically changing weather patterns, creating challenges (flash floods) & opportunities (rain-induced traffic).



    *  **Regional Biome Customization**: The game world itself should adapt to region; create a dynamic landscape affecting gameplay.


13.  **Monetizable Models:**

     *   Cosmetics – Clothing, accessories related to historical/mythological groups -  minor cosmetic unlocks for character progression.


      * Bundles: Specialised cosmetics will also offer advantages in gameplay (e.g., "Spice Mastery" which provides extra buffs)



14 . **Future Considerations** (Long Term Expansion Ideas):


     * Enhanced Scripting Tool: Allow greater customization, allowing level designers to tweak specific events and story branches more readily



    * Modding Support : Enable player modding: Allowing players to expand the world by modifying new events, quests or even building cities.
