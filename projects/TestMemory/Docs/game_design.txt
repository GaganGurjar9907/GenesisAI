## Game Design Documents – Speed Riders (Racing Simulator)

**Document Version**: 1.0
 **Release Target:** Spring 2024
**Target Audience:** PC Gaming, Mobile (planned), Fans of racing games, Simulation enthusiasts


---

**(1)  Introduction**

* **Game Title:** Speeders - Rival Trails
* **Genre**: Racing Simulator - Arcade Elements & Progression Focus. Blending skillful driving with charming retro-vibes and engaging race management elements 
* **Platform(s):** PC (Steam), Mobile (iOS and Android – slated)
*   **Main Theme/Tone:** Energetic, Fast-Paced, Nostalgic, Chaotic. Imagine a blend of Gran Turismo Neo and Need:ED's arcade racing gameplay with a colorful character-driven story and unique track design variations. 

**(2) Executive Summary & Goals**

Speeders – Rival Trails is an action-racing game designed to deliver incredibly addictive yet approachable experiences.  The core concept centers on customizing and building racing careers centered around challenging, dynamic courses. The game focuses heavily on player skill, strategic decision-making *and* a charming story of escalating rivalry amongst young racers in the rural countryside. Key goals:

1. **High Fluidity & Reactive Driving**: Prioritize believable and responsive controls for comfortable and captivating driving.
2. **Unique Multiplayer Modes:** Provide diverse, engaging, and competitive multiplayer options (ranked and unranked). 

3.  **Charming, Visually Appealing Aesthetics:**  Employ a stylistic "vintage speed racer" aesthetic with vibrant colors, stylized graphics, and audio to create an immersive experience.


**(3) Core Gameplay Mechanics**

* **Racing Variety**:
    * **Time Trials**: Focused on consistent lap times. 
    * **Arena Races**:  Multiple tracks, varying levels of difficulty & challenges like speed-based obstacles, drift zones, and time-wasting elements.
    * **Free Mode (Race Against AI)**: A classic race against AI. Difficulty escalates based on time/location, aiming for a challenging challenge. 

* **Character Progression:**
     *  Players start as rookies and progress to specialist racers focused around vehicle type (e.g., 'Stunt Rider', ‘Muscle Car’, ‘Tracker’) with distinct base stats & abilities tied to character customization.
     *   **Stats:** Momentum, Speed, Handling, Top Speed, Drift Ability, Brake Hardness

* **Course Mastery (Simulated):**
    1.  Each track is broken down into zones/regions and requires mastery across multiple difficulty levels to obtain new zones/areas as you level up.
    2.  The race begins after a short tutorial. Focus will be on mastering the control scheme of the track itself
3. **Race Management:** (Adding in later releases)
       * Mini-Games within races: Short scenarios that introduce specific mechanics impacting speed or drifting




**(4) World/Environment Details**

* **Setting**: Rural Montana/Western America (inspired by Americana).  Lots of scenic, winding roads and large open areas featuring varied landscapes. 
 * **Visual style:** Retro color palette transitioning to a stylized modern look. Include elements like snowdrifts in winter/autumn and sunsets. 



**(5) Game Modes**

* **Story Mode**: A light narrative campaign around the rivalry amongst five young racers. The main story guides the player through various race challenges and world events leading into a larger unveiling of the game's main mystery.
*  **Rival Race (2v2)**: Rival races between 5 unique players using different character customizations focusing on fast-paced strategic combat.

**(6) World Map/Progression & Progression Loop - Detailed Breakdown:**


* **Tutorial Zone**: A short tutorial world guiding the new player through core mechanics.
  * Introduction of characters and basic car controls.


*   **Era Levels - 3 Zones**: Level design expands into progressively bigger, more challenging tracks.

    * Zones Detail: Each zone (level) combines elements of previous zones with added complexity & challenges for experienced racers.


* **Rival Race Worldmap:** Players earn rewards/points upon racing and can race opponents. Reward progression is awarded across the map for completing races & levels.



**(7)  Specific Features - Tech & Design**

*   **Multiplayer System**: 
    * Competitive Ladder – Structured competitive leaderboards based on race performance.
 * **Race Customization:** Players can purchase cosmetic and thematic upgrade kits which affect vehicle handling and look across the world map.
     * **Vehicle Variety:** Different vehicle types, each with distinct driving characteristics

   * Character customization: As characters progress to higher levels, they are able to personalize their appearance, name, race profile etc.. 



**8) Art Style & Animation**


*   **2D/Stylized 3D Hybrid**: Utilizing a slightly animated/comic-book inspired aesthetic with distinct character sprites
    * **Animation Style:** Loose but expressive animation focused on emphasizing fast action.

---



**(9) Monetization - Considerations (Prioritized)**


*  Premium Model(s): Single purchase for the full experience. (Highly prioritised!) 
*   Cosmetic Packs : (Seasonal - will be available with launch). Offers themed character appearances and vehicle customization items. Focused purely on cosmetic elements to increase appeal



**(10) Technology Stack**

* **Game Engines:** Unity, Unreal Engine (depending on development pace/scale) 
* **Programming Languages**: C#, Blueprint scripting (Unity or Unreal),  C++ (where applicable)




---

**Timeline & Stages - (High Level)**
(Initial Project - MVP Launch):

* Phase 1 (6 Months): Concept Definition, Art Style Development, Core Mechanics Implementation, Engine Setup.
* Phase 2 (4-6 months): Early Arena Race Testing, Prototype Refinement,  Multiplayer Beginnings
* Phase 3 (2-3 months) Balancing/Polishing Gameplay, Initial Content Release (Early Zones, Tutorial Zone).
* Phased Iteration:  Regular content updates, feature additions, bug fixes based on player feedback.
